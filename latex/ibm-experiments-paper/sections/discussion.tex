\section{Discussion}

Results demonstrate:
\begin{itemize}
    \item high-fidelity entanglement on Heron R2,
    \item strong reproducibility across independent runs,
    \item clear demonstration of the quantum no-cloning theorem via BB84,
    \item high entropy and randomness in QRNG,
    \item statistically significant ground-state bias in VQC.
\end{itemize}
