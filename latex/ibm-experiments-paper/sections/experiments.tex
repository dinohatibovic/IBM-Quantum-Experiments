\section{Experiments}

\subsection{Bell State Entanglement}
A standard Bell circuit was executed with 1,000 shots. Fidelity, concurrence, and tangle were computed.

\subsection{Reproducibility Test}
Two independent runs of the same circuit were compared using chi-squared tests.

\subsection{BB84 Quantum Key Distribution}
Alice, Eve, and Bob roles were implemented. QBER was measured under eavesdropping.

\subsection{Variational Quantum Circuit}
A 2-qubit VQC with 10 parameters and tied weights was trained. Entropy and chi-squared metrics were computed.

\subsection{Quantum Random Number Generation}
8-bit outputs were collected and analyzed for uniqueness and entropy.
