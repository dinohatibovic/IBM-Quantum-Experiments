\begin{abstract}
This work presents eight verified quantum computing experiments executed on IBM Quantum hardware (ibm\_fez Heron R2, 156 qubits; ibm\_torino Heron R1, 133 qubits) between January 27 and March 15, 2026. A total of 39,010 quantum measurements were collected across Bell state entanglement, BB84 quantum key distribution, variational quantum circuits, quantum random number generation, and reproducibility tests. All results were decoded from raw SamplerV2 BitArray archives using a zlib + base64 + NumPy pipeline, ensuring full reproducibility. Statistical validation includes Wilson confidence intervals, chi-squared tests, and entropy analysis. No simulations were used.
\end{abstract}
